Our Story

More Than a Meal,
A Memory

Born in the heart of Vijayawada, India's Largest Restaurant began as a dream to unite India's most beloved flavors under one magnificent roof. From the spice trails of Andhra to the aromatic kitchens of Punjab, our menu is a culinary journey across the subcontinent.

Nestled in Poranki, we have welcomed over a million guests, crafting unforgettable dining experiences that blend tradition with contemporary elegance. Our award-winning chefs source the finest local ingredients, transforming them into dishes that tell a story.
